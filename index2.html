What is biopocket?

A FREE tool, within everyone's reach A real coach personalized biodiversity which fits in the pocket!

Map

The points on the map shows the location of different actions that are done or are under progress by each user.

Action of Construction

This is a construction action of building a bee hotel. You can see the starting date, tasks, and latest status.

Planting

This action consists of three tasks: buying the seeds, buying the fertlizer, and watering the seeds.

Observation

Observation is the act of taking a photo of a species(usually a rare one), uploading the photo in the application, and adding some hashtags about the characteristics of the species.

Dashboard

A dashboard, to manage each task and have a regular follow up; the opportunity to engage to perform tasks at your own pace

pour motiver et encourager chaque utilisateur dans ses démarches en faveur de la biodiversité.

Tout au long de la découverte, c’est un dossier virtuel d’observations que chacun se construit, pour, par exemple, les consulter et les partager plus tard.

By clicking on each point, you can see the user who is doing the action, the starting and ending date, and the tasks to be followed for each specific action.

To build a bee hotel there are three tasks to be done: buying the wax, planting a tree, and building the hotel.

Other users have the possibility to add or correct the information about the uploaded photo by leaving a comment.
